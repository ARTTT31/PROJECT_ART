# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ART Workspace is used by internal operators and admins who need a compact dashboard for calendar activity, task lists, oil prices, QR creation, and related workspace tools.

## Product Purpose

The product gives users a fast, trustworthy operational dashboard where important widgets can be scanned, rearranged, and shown or hidden without friction.

## Brand Personality

Professional, calm, premium. The interface should feel polished and dependable without becoming decorative or distracting.

## Anti-references

Avoid loud marketing visuals, heavy decoration, novelty controls, low-contrast gray text, and dashboard elements that feel playful at the expense of clarity.

## Design Principles

- Keep controls familiar, but make state obvious.
- Use restrained color for active selections and primary actions.
- Preserve dashboard density while giving important controls enough breathing room.
- Favor clear Thai labels and direct action copy.
- Make interactive states visible for keyboard, mouse, and touch users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Respect reduced motion preferences, maintain keyboard access, and keep touch targets at least 44px.
